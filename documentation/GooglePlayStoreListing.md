## Full description

**Don’t Tap: Rogue Op** is a focus game dressed as a rogue system test: you are the Operator. One hand stays on the **Focus Node**; everything else on screen is trying to break your concentration.

**Core loop**
- **Hold** the Focus Node at the bottom of the screen until the **countdown** hits zero.
- **Do not release** early — that is a containment breach.
- **Do not tap** floating **Anomalies** (fake warnings, buttons, banners). One wrong tap fails the sequence.

**Threats (moving hazards)**  
Glowing **yellow threats** drift in from the edges. If one reaches your Focus Node, the system **shaves time off the clock** and shakes the run; if the timer hits zero, the sequence fails.

While still holding the node, **swipe** up, down, left, or right to fire a short **pulse** in that direction. Pulses **destroy real threats** on contact. You only have **limited pulse charges** per sequence (fewer charges on harder tiers), so timing matters. Destroying a threat can **recover a charge** back toward your max.

**Decoys (red “virus” orbs)**  
Some incoming objects are **decoys** (red glow, **pulsing**, with a clear **X** marking). If a decoy **reaches** your Focus Node it **fades away harmlessly** — no time loss. The trap is **firing a pulse into a decoy**: that **costs a large chunk of time**, adds extra pressure on the countdown, and can end the run if the timer hits zero. **Only pulse the yellow threats.**

**Difficulty & sequences**  
Sequences get **longer**, anomalies spawn **faster**, threats move **quicker**, and more threats can be on screen at once as you advance. Past the first tier, **decoys** can appear more often with steeper penalties.

**Operator ranks (persistent)**  
Your best completed sequence feeds an **Operator Rank** (shown on the main terminal and in the **Restraint Index**). Ranks are titles like Trainee Operator up through System Architect. **Reaching higher sequences unlocks new ranks** — and higher ranks **turn on extra “system modifiers”** during play, for example:
- anomalies that **change direction** mid-flight,
- a **flickering** Focus Node outline,
- brief **screen corruption**,
- **mimic** objects that look like the real node,
- **glitched timer** readouts,
- and **fake system overlays** that try to fool you.

Top rank runs with **everything stacked** for maximum deception.

**Progress you keep (local only)**  
The **Restraint Index** tracks **highest sequence reached**, **total time endured**, your **current rank**, and progress toward the next rank. All of this stays **on your device** — no account, no online leaderboard in this version.

**Comfort options**  
**Sound effects** and **haptic feedback** can be turned off in **System Calibration** (settings). Pause is available during a run; after resume you re-hold the node and get a short **re-engage countdown** before chaos returns.

**Straight facts**
- Works **offline**.
- **No account** required.
- **No ads** in this version.
- Flashing / urgent UI-style visuals and tension — suited for players who enjoy that style of challenge.
