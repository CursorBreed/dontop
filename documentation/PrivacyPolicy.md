## 2. Summary — what we collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on our servers.**  
The App is designed to work **offline**. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or other personal identifiers for the App to function.

---

## 3. Information stored only on your device (local storage)

To provide basic game functionality, the App may store **non-personal, gameplay-related data locally on your device** using the device’s standard app storage (for example: highest level reached, total time played, sound and haptics preferences). This data:

- Stays **on your device** unless you remove the App or clear the App’s data through your device settings.
- Is **not** sent to Tinu Oni Bata Enterprises, **not** uploaded to our servers, and **not** used by us for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

We cannot access this local data unless you choose to contact us and voluntarily share information (for example, in a support email).

---

## 4. No analytics, advertising, or sale of data (as provided in the App)

As released, **Don’t Tap: Rogue Op** does not include third-party advertising SDKs or analytics that collect personal data for us. We **do not sell** personal information.

If we materially change this in a future version (for example, adding analytics or online features), we will update this Privacy Policy and, where required, the app store disclosures (such as Google Play’s Data safety section) before or when those features go live.

## 5. Permissions

The App may request only what is needed for normal operation on your device (for example, access related to audio playback or vibration, depending on your Android version and settings). We do not use permissions to harvest personal data for transmission to us.
