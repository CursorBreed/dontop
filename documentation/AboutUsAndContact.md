# About Us & Contact — Don’t Tap: Rogue Op

## About us

**Don’t Tap: Rogue Op** is published by **Tinu Oni Bata Enterprises**, a small studio based in Lagos, Nigeria. We build focused mobile games that are easy to pick up but hard to master.

In **Don’t Tap: Rogue Op**, you are an Operator holding a single **Focus Node** while a rogue system throws fake warnings, buttons, and distractions your way. Lift your finger or tap the wrong thing and it’s a containment breach. The game is **offline**, **free of ads** (as shipped), and built for short, intense sessions that test calm and focus—not reflex spam.

We believe privacy matters: the game does not require an account and we do not collect your personal data on our servers. Progress lives on your device so you can play without signing in.
